Sorter for Ace Attorney characters. Pick your games, and hit the Start button.

Click on the character you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally.

Depending on how many sources you choose, you could get up to 700+ picks, so prepare to be here a while!



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress). 1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.
